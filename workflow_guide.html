‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Does the policy learn during evaluation?
NO! Evaluation is read-only testing. The policy only learns during training in run_phase2.html. Think of it like: Training = studying, Evaluation = taking a test.
Q: How does "evolution" work?
When you import a policy and train more, it plays against itself using its current knowledge. As it plays, it discovers which moves lead to wins against SMART opponents (itself), not just random. Over time, it learns deeper strategies like "tile 7 is bad on hex 4 in the opening, but great on hex 11 mid-game."
Q: How many times can I re-train the same policy?
Unlimited! Just keep importing the latest policy.json and running more generations. Each time it gets smarter. Some AIs are trained for millions of games.
Q: What if my policy stops improving?
This is called "convergence" - it's learned as much as it can with current method. Solutions: 1) Increase exploration rate to try new things, 2) Add opponent diversity, 3) Train against other strong policies, or 4) You've created a master AI!
Q: Should I save every generation's policy?
YES! Save policies every 5-10 generations. This lets you: 1) Compare improvement over time, 2) Roll back if training goes wrong, 3) Create tournaments, 4) Share different strength AIs with others.
Q: What's a realistic timeline to a strong AI?
‚Ä¢ 5K games: Beats random (60-70%)
‚Ä¢ 20K games: Decent strategy, understands openings
‚Ä¢ 50K games: Strong player, good tactics
‚Ä¢ 100K+ games: Expert level, near-optimal play
Q: Can I train overnight?
Yes! Set "Custom" mode with many generations. At ~200 games/sec, you can do 50K games in ~4 minutes. Overnight could be 500K+ games!
